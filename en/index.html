"HOT YOGA" AND ITS BENEFITS

El "HOT YOGA" is recreated through a room with a heating system at controlled temperatures, which generate a sensation of warmth or increase its intensity, ranging between 30 and 40 degrees Celsius depending on the class.

Your body is purified through sweat as you go deeper into each posture and your mind remains FOCUS, a great benefit provided by the connection with the heat in your body.

"HOT YOGA" has multiple benefits for your health, through sweating your pores open and a process of eliminating toxins begins, your vital energy rises, your concentration potential increases and you feel a deep connection with your body, achieving a state of calm and mental stillness, releasing stress, muscle tension, making your body more flexible, strengthening your immune system, improving strength and endurance.

A physical challenge that transforms YOUR BEING in a comprehensive way.

YOGA STYLES AND MORE...

Passive Static Yoga

Passive static yoga styles offer a more leisurely and focused way of practicing, allowing for greater introspection, deep relaxation, strengthening and flexibility of the body, promoting an overall balance between body and mind.

Rhythmic Dynamic Yoga

Dynamic styles of yoga tend to be more vigorous and fluid, physically challenging compared to static styles.

Powerful Intense Yoga

Power styles of yoga tend to be more intense and physically challenging compared to other styles.

STRETCHING AND MOBILITY

For those who do not feel an affinity with yoga, we have designed STRETCHING AND MOBILITY classes within our services, created to deeply stretch your body and receive its bodily benefits through targeted stretching..
